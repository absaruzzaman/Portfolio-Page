As a software engineering student with a growing passion for UI/UX design, I’ve been balancing my studies and projects while gradually shifting my focus towards the world of design. Although I’ve worked on various AI and development projects, I’ve come to realize that designing intuitive and user-friendly interfaces excites me the most. Understanding user behavior and crafting seamless experiences is where I feel I can truly make an impact.

At the same time, I’m still committed to building a solid technical foundation, diving deeper into cybersecurity, operating systems, and computer networks. I believe that understanding these areas will make me a more versatile designer, giving me a broader perspective on how different systems interact with the front-end user experience.

Balancing both my technical studies and my design ambitions has been challenging but rewarding. While I aim to become a successful UI/UX designer, I’m also keen on keeping my cybersecurity knowledge sharp, as it’s an area I find intriguing, especially in the Python domain.
